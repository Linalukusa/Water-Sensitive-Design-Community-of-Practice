Water Sensitive Urban Design Community of Practice

The South African Water Sensitive Design Community of Practice facilitates the widespread implementation of WSD through knowledge sharing, capacity development and stakeholder engagement activities.

Water Sensitive Design

It is the integration of water cycle management, planning and design for the growth and development of urban, peri-urban and rural environments.
